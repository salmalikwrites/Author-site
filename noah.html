Noah

The Chained Sapling - The boy whom which fate revolves.

Who she is

A boy fractured by choices he never made. He doesn’t yet know who he is, but everyone he meets is certain of what he must become. What is a life shaped by other people’s decisions?

Exclusive Snippet (childhood)

“Drip. The water fell again, sliding into his hair, soaking it with the cold he once knew too well. The children stared at the strands stained white. He was different. He had always been different.”
