Alice

The Ash-Crowned Heir — power gathered, identity split.

Who she is

She was born beneath a lion’s sigil and raised to be its blade. Power obeys her, yet her name fractures in the mirror — thief of gifts, taker of souls, a daughter sharpening herself against the throne she intends to break. Freedom has a price, and she has counted the cost: her father’s life.

Exclusive Snippet (childhood)

The halo over the altar was cracked. She stood on a stool and touched the fracture. Ash flaked onto her palm like snow that had forgotten how to shine. “Mine,” she whispered — not the halo, not the church — the future. Somewhere far away, a lion roared. The sound lived in her bones and would not leave.
