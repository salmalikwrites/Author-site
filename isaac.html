Isaac

The Antlered Healer — a storm held in his hands.

Who he is

A healer with careful hands and a ledger no one sees. Brown-haired, soft-spoken, he stitches wounds while arranging his own betrayals. The world calls him merciful; only he knows the lightning waiting beneath his calm. Restoration is his craft — and his mask.

Exclusive Snippet (childhood)

“Hold still,” he whispered, palms hovering over the splintered antler. Green motes lifted from his fingertips — shy fireflies — drifting into the cracks as if they remembered where bone should be. Somewhere beyond the trees, thunder murmured. He pretended not to hear it.
