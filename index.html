About

Sal is a novelist and poet based in the UK. Current projects include Endbringer (a genre-bending epic) and In the Shadows of Freedom (a horror-tinged myth). Influences: Milton, Blake, Dante, and the stubborn hope that craft still matters.

Elsewhere: playing ball, tutoring maths, and building an online home for long-form work.
